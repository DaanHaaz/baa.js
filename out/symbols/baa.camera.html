Class Detail
baa.camera(x, y, width, height)
A tool that helps you with cameras in your game. Make the camera follow an object, decide the size of the camera, and give it boundaries. You can also zoom in and rotate.
Parameters:
{number} x
The horizontal position of the camera on the screen.
{number} y
The vertical position of the camera on the screen.
{number} width
The width of the camera.
{number} height
The height of the camera.
Field Detail

The object the camera should follow.
<private> {baa.point} _real
The actual position of the camera, meaning the x position + the shake offset.
<private> {baa.point} _shakeOffset
The offset of the camera shake.
<private> {number} _shakePower
The power of the camera shake. The higher the number, the more the camera shakes.
<private> {baa.timer} _shakeTimer
How long the screenshake takes.
<private> {baa.point} _velocity
The velocity of the camera. This is based on the distance of the object.
<private> {baa.rect} _window
The rectangle everything is drawn in.
<private> {baa.rect} _world
The world's boundaries. The camera won't go out of this.
{boolean} active
If the camera is active. Set it to false to stop moving and drawing with the camera.
{number} speed
How fast the camera moves.
{number} zoom
How far the camera is zoomed in. 1 means not zoomed in at all.

<static> baa.camera.shake(power, time)
Shakes the camera.
Parameters:
{number} power Optional, Default: 3
How much the screen should shake. Use a number higher than 0. If 0, shaking currently going on will stop. If null, The default will be used.
{number} time Optional, Default: 0.3
How long the camera should shake.
